COLLABORATION STRATEGY & RECOMMENDATIONS
===================================================================

TOP 10 HIGH-PRIORITY CAFES FOR IMMEDIATE OUTREACH:
1. Amethyst Cafe - Premium, hosts cultural events, boutique space
2. Spaces/Synck - Dedicated art/performance venue, beach location
3. Writer's Cafe - Literary theme, social cause, creative crowd
4. The Board Game Lounge - Already hosts events, gaming + craft synergy
5. Backyard Night Market - Community space, workshops, pop-ups
6. Ciclo Cafe - 4 locations, community-focused, spacious
7. Shiraz Art Cafe - Art-focused, creative community
8. The Brew Room - 10+ years, established creative community
9. Episodes Cafe - Unique private theater concept, special events
10. Chamiers Cafe - Upscale, shopping complex, boutique synergy

COLLABORATION IDEAS TO PITCH:
✓ Monthly "Crochet & Coffee" workshops
✓ Display and sell products in boutique/retail space
✓ Beginner crochet classes for cafe customers
✓ Custom crochet decor for cafe (coasters, plant holders, wall art)
✓ Joint Instagram collaborations and giveaways
✓ "Craft Sundays" or themed craft events
✓ Pop-up shops during cafe events
✓ Crochet + coffee subscription packages
✓ DIY crochet kits available at cafe counter
✓ Seasonal craft workshops (holiday decorations, gifts)

CONTACT STRATEGY:
1. Instagram DM first - introduce Boop the Yarn, share portfolio
2. Follow up with email if available (within 3-5 days)
3. Propose trial collaboration (free mini-workshop or product display)
4. Emphasize mutual benefits: creative community + venue/audience
5. Offer to create custom pieces for their space as introduction
6. Suggest starting small (one workshop) before committing to series

BEST TIMES TO REACH OUT:
✓ Weekday mornings: 10 AM - 12 PM (cafe managers available)
✓ Avoid weekends (busy with customers)
✓ Best months: January-March, July-September (event planning seasons)
✓ Avoid: December (holiday rush), April-May (summer slow season)

PRICING SUGGESTIONS FOR WORKSHOPS:
- Beginner workshop (2 hours): ₹800-1200 per person
- Materials included: ₹1200-1500 per person
- Private group workshop: ₹15,000-25,000 (10-15 people)
- Cafe revenue share: 60-40 or 70-30 split
- Product display: 20-30% commission to cafe

INSTAGRAM ENGAGEMENT STRATEGY:
1. Follow all cafes from @booptheyarn
2. Like and comment on posts for 1-2 weeks
3. Tag cafes in your crochet work posts
4. Share their content in stories
5. Create "dream collaboration" content featuring their spaces
6. Use location tags when visiting cafes
7. Build relationship before pitching

ADDITIONAL RESEARCH NOTES:
✓ Chennai has growing indoor art workshop culture (pottery, resin, mandala)
✓ Cafes increasingly host creative workshops and community events
✓ Target audience: Young professionals, students, creative community, 20-40 age
✓ Instagram is primary communication channel for Chennai cafes
✓ Many cafes have event spaces or can accommodate 10-20 people
✓ Weekend workshops (Saturday/Sunday 11am-1pm or 3pm-5pm) most popular
✓ Coworking cafes good for weekday evening workshops (6pm-8pm)
✓ Beach area cafes attract tourists - good for one-time workshops
✓ Residential area cafes (Anna Nagar, Adyar) - good for regular classes
